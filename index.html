Band's Article

GIRLS ON FLEEK is a rap band in training, formed by three teenage girls :
  • Eniola Isibor
  • Tari Steve Johnson
  • Musibau Halidah
But they also have stage names:
  • Ennie Pearl
  • Lil Ari
  • Ehldie
They formed in October 2016 at Abuja, Nigeria. they have been together for five years, they adore Korea a lot so they choose to follow the system of K-pop groups but just slightly different.

Girls on Fleek also call themselves "THE RAP TRIANGLE". Once they debut, they will call their fans "FLEEKERS". They are hoping to debut as soon as they graduate from school with their song,"Vibes".
Membership roles of Girls on Fleek :

Ennie Pearl : The lead rapper, vocal substitute & yongest Member

Ehldie : The lead rapper, vocal substitute and main song writer

Lil Ari : The main rapper, main visual & olderst member
